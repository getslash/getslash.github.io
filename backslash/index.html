Backslash

All of Your Test Results, in One Place

All Test Results in One Place

Backslash is a test reporting service that keeps track of all test executions, their results and additional details. It enables you to stay on top of the overall testing efforts in your organization, and make sense of it all.

Drill Down with Ease

Backslash saves all the important details about each running session, including custom metadata reported from custom tools.

Error Reporting in Detail

Exceptions and errors in tests are diligently recorded, enabling you to analyze failures quickly and efficiently

Backslash also keeps track of warnings emitted from test sessions, emphasizing even the smallest of problems
