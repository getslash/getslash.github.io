Backslash

All of Your Test Results, in One Place

What is Backslash?

Backslash is a centralized test reporting and tracking service. It allows you to keep track of ongoing automated test execution, follow ongoing issues, and get a better estimate of your project's code quality.

Track Session Execution -- in one Place

Backslash stores automation session information efficiently. It allows you to backtrack execution status - including successful runs, skips, errors and more.

Investigate Errors Efficiently

Backslash allows you to take a quick glance at errors, spot non-issues with ease, and drill deep into the root cause of failed tests and sessions.

Backslash captures failure and exception tracebacks from code and displays them, allowing you to investigate without leaving your chair.

Moderate Results

Stay on top of your regression tests -- sort real issues from false positives. You can archive sessions you decide are not relevant for your testing effort, and you can quickly mark failures as investigated, avoiding unnecessary churn

Backslash also allows you to comment on tests and sessions, giving you a place to preserve the information you extract
